Safety

As gaming becomes more social, gaming companies need a partner who can help them in creating a safe, productive environment for their users. CleanSpeak prevents problem users from disrupting the game and driving away players. Eliminate profanity and hate speech from your gaming environment at enterprise scale.

"Video game developers must make note that toxicity in game communities is still an issue for our industry. Moderation systems, positive culture cultivation, and exclusion of the worst offenders is necessary to allow everyone to enjoy gaming. Management of communities is even more important with the increase in online gaming."

- Renee Gittins, Executive Director of IGDA

Cyberbullying

CleanSpeak has a stringent "denylist" of words associated with bullying (e.g., "fat" or "hate"). An intelligent real-time filter allows a moderator to add words and phrases to make sure it is kept up to date with current slang used by kids and teens.

Hate Speech

Gaming platforms cannot risk having hate speech throughout their communities. CleanSpeak filters hate speech in real time in over 15 languages with tremendous flexibility for our partners to make sure their communities are safe.

Usernames and PII

Preventing users from sharing account information is a security concern as well as a way to prevent paid accounts from being shared. When hosting a virtual environment targeted to kids, you are also required to take reasonable measures to prevent users from sharing personal identifiable information in accordance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act. CleanSpeak makes these efforts easy.
